About

Every track tells a Story; every sound holds a Soul.

Hi there,

I'm Ramana, an independent music producer, composer, and singer hailing from the cultural heart of Tamil Nadu, I bridge emotional raw lyricism with contemporary, alternative, and cinematic soundscapes. My goal is to craft independent Tamil music that moves beyond just a vibe, it’s an atmosphere you experience. Whether it's Tamil film music, heavy orchestral compositions, or iconic soundscapes , I'm here to build a new wave of independent sound with contemporary, pop, R&B, and classical tradition.

As a composer, producer, singer, and songwriter, Ramana brings an intimate honesty to every track — blending cinematic depth with raw emotions and classical touches. It will speaks across languages and genres, rooted in culture yet reaching beyond borders.
